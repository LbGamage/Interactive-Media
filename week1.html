— HUMAN COMPUTER IN YOUR FACE —

In this workshop, we put together a gesture glossary by noting down different movements and behaviors that triggered interactions between people and non-human systems. We looked at how simple gestures could act as commands or signals in human-computer interaction.

The hand gesture of crocheting involves a repetitive motion of looping yarn over a hooked needle, with one hand guiding the yarn while the other maneuvers the hook in small, rhythmic movements to create stitches.
