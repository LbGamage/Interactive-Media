Week 2

In Week 2, we took a journey through the different versions of the WWW: Web 1.0, the static web; Web 2.0, the social web; and Web 3.0, the 3D decentralized web. We also explored various types of websites. During the workshop we did some Paper Prototyping, trying out Crazy 8s and creating wireframes for our workbook. Along with that we got into the basics of CSS, covering layout, units, fonts, and more!

/More Ideation at Home

My favourite out of the 8 was the bookshelf and I explored more variations of it after I went home. I really wanted to make a workbook that represented my personality well. Something related to trinkets, becasue I love collecting things. After multiple cycles of ideating I ended up choosing a pin-board as I felt like it would be more fun than a bookshelf. Organized clutter is one of my favourite things and I hope to show it through the homepage as i get better at coding.

After completing the Crazy 8s exercise and further brainstorming at home, I decided on a pinboard-style homepage. In this design, specific items on the board serve as interactive elements that lead to corresponding pages. Each page follows a theme related to the clicked image/item: for example, a polaroid of a sunset might connect to Week 2 or maybe it could feature ASCII art.
With this concept in mind, I experimented with paper prototyping to determine which items should be interactive. I then sketched a rough draft of what I want the homepage layout to look like.
