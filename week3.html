Responsive Design

Responsive Design allows people to access content across multiple device resolutions. The viewports should adjust logically on tablets, phones, and desktops of any resolution. Adopt a mobile-first approach, utilize scalable vector graphics (SVGs) for icons and illustrations, and implement three or more breakpoints for various screen sizes.
There are 3 main principles to follow:

  • Grid Systems: Uses relative sizing instead of fixed pixel dimensions.
  • Fluid Image: Images resize dynamically to fit their containers.
  • Media Queries:Adjust layouts based on screen size breakpoints.

Way Back Machine

A digital archive of the World Wide Web, maintained by the Internet Archive, allowing users to access archived versions of websites from the past

The Uncensored Library

A library within Minecraft, created by Reporters Without Borders and BlockWorks, designed to circumvent censorship and provide access to banned articles and journalism from countries with limited press freedom

The Met Collection

Digital archive of The Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York. It features over 490,000 artworks spanning 5,000 years of history, including paintings, sculptures, textiles, decorative arts, and more.
