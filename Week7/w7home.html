In this project, we built a simple touch-sensitive circuit using open wires and a buzzer. When we touched the exposed ends of the wires, the buzzer would activate. When we complete the circuit by touching the wires we allow a small electrical current to flow, which triggers the buzzer to make sound. To make it even more entertaining, we stuck the wires into different potatoes. When we tapped on the potatoes, it would play musical notes through the buzzer.
